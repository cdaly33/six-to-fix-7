**Client Delivery — The Full Production Reality**

**The Core Delivery Model**

Every client runs on the same engine: **Research In → Strategy Synthesized → Content and Messaging Out → Measured → Adjusted.** The 6 To Fix framework is the organizing logic. The volume of output is what most people underestimate. A single active client at $4,000–5,000/month could be generating 30–50 distinct content (high) and messaging assets per month across all six areas. Here's what that actually looks like.

**Stage 1: Research (Month 1 for Every Client)**

This is the foundation everything else is built on. Get it wrong and you're producing content that sounds good but doesn't connect.

**AI fully handles:**

* Competitor content audit — scrape and analyze 5–10 competitor websites, their messaging, offers, and positioning (Perplexity + Claude)
* Industry trend synthesis — pull market reports, trade press, category trends into a digestible brief
* Audience persona drafts from intake data, industry data, and analogous business research
* SWOT analysis draft from inputs you gather in discovery calls
* Existing content audit — categorize and grade what the client already has
* Google Business Profile, review sentiment analysis, and NPS language mining
* SEO keyword landscape by buyer segment and intent stage

**Human essential (you):**

* A 60-90-minute recorded client discovery call (you run it, but a VA transcribes and timestamps)
* Any primary research that requires phone interviews with the client's actual customers (hire a research contractor for 3–5 customer interviews— this data is gold
* The strategic synthesis. AI gives you ingredients. You write the diagnosis. What's broken, what's the priority, what's the angle. This is the clinical judgment that justifies your retainer.
* The client kickoff conversation where you translate research into a clear strategic POV they haven't heard before

**Output of Month 1:** A 6 To Fix Strategy Brief — 15–20 pages covering each area with findings, strategic priorities, and content/messaging direction. AI drafts 70% of it. You edit, sharpen, and inject the strategic narrative.

**What You Can't Fully Automate (Be Honest About This)**

The system handles production. It doesn't replace judgment. Three things still need you:

**Strategic diagnosis** — Looking at all the research and deciding what the actual problem is and what to fix first. AI can generate analysis. You make the call.

**Client relationship management** — The monthly call, the moments where a client is frustrated or excited or uncertain. No system replaces that.

**Quality gate** — You are the final filter on everything that goes to a client. The system makes that review fast (15–20 minutes per piece vs. 2–3 hours of production), but you never skip it.
